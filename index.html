Fun facts about rabbits!

  • Rabbits have a blind spot right in-between their eyes.
  • Male rabbits are called Bucks, while females are does. Babies are called kits.
  • Rabbits don't have paw pads! They only have thick cushions of fur beneath their feet.
  • There are currently 45 known breeds of rabbits.
